How do bees collect honey?

When plants bloom, then the honey bees collect nectar from the flowers of those plants. They suck the nectar using their tongues and store it in their stomach. The nectar mixes with the enzymes and proteins in bee's stomach and gets converted to honey. The bees then drop the honey in their combs.
